Fishing Statistics

These statistics pages show several reports which display details of fish caught over a 2 year period from 2019 to 2020

  • Fish by species by year : Number of fish caught by fish species for each of the years referenced above
  • Fish by venue by year : Number of fish caught at each venue for each of the years referenced above
  • Fish by bait by year : Number of fish caught with each bait for each of the years referenced above
  • Fish records : The record weight for each fish type for each year
  • Top twenty trips : A listing of the top twenty trips in both fish numbers and fish weight
  • Trips by venue by year : A listing of the total number of trips to each venue per year

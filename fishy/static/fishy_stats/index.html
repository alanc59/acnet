Fishy Statistics

These statistics pages show several reports which display details of fish caught over a 2 year period from 2019 to 2020

  • Fish By Species By Year : Number of different type of fish divided by fish species for each of the years referenced above
  • Fish By Venue By Year : Number of fish caught at each venue for each of the years referenced above
  • Fish Records : The record weight for each fish type for each year
